Admin Panel/ Call Centre

Notes

As an Admin, I can log in to the admin panel using administrator credentials.

 

 

As an Admin, I can browse/modify/block customer and business accounts.

 

As an Admin, I can review documents/certificates in requests submitted by businesses and change their profiles accordingly.

 

As an Admin, I can review and edit job requests submitted by customers.

 

 

As an Admin, I can make refunds to a customer or Tradie

 

As an Admin, I can review and edit quotes from Tradies.

 

As an Admin, I can browse/edit/delete reviews.

 

As an Admin, I can initiate password recovery for customer and business accounts.

 

As an Admin, I can browse the articles list, create/edit/delete articles.

 

As an Admin, I can see statistics. Eg

●     Data on how long it take for a job lead to be sold out

●     Data on what type of jobs in order or requested

 

As an Admin, I can create users and admins and assign roles to them.

 

As an Admin, I can view the number of tradies per area and the number of jobs per area

 

As an Admin, I can search customer profiles by email, name, phone, address and browse their profiles and history

 

As an Admin, I can search business profiles by name, phone, industry/service types, location and browse their profile, history.

 

As an Admin,I can send email/sms notifications/messages to customers and businesses.

 

As an Admin, I can access the online support chat interface as an operator.
